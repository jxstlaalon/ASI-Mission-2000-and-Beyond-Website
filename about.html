ABOUT US

Founder Information

Marvalee Franklyn is the chief coordinator and a leader of the ASI Mission 2000 and Beyond initiative. She works with a team to lead this inter-generational, mission-focused ministry, which involves children and young people in evangelism and mission impact.

  • Leader: Marvalee Franklyn
  • Role: Chief coordinator and leader of the mission
  • Mission: A ministry that aims to disciple children and youth through evangelism and mission projects, led by Franklyn and her team

Our Founder's Journey

Marvalee Franklyn was moved to create ASI Mission 2000 and Beyond after witnessing the transformative power of youth engagement in community service during a mission trip to rural communities in 1998. Seeing firsthand how young people could make a significant impact when given proper guidance and opportunities inspired her to establish a structured ministry.

With over 20 years of experience in youth ministry and community development, Marvalee recognized the need for an organization that would not only provide mission opportunities but also foster spiritual growth and leadership skills in the next generation. Her vision was to create a sustainable model that would continue to impact communities for years to come.

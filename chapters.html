Chapters

ASI Mission 2000 and Beyond are made of many different chapters / contingents from many different countries.

Barbados (Headquarters)

Chapter Leader: Marvalee Franklyn

Year Formed: 2000

Average Members: 30-50 (Core Committee)

Additional Information: ASI was founded in Barbados by Sister Marvalee Franklyn in 2000. Barbados is the home and birthplace of ASI Mission 2000 and beyond. As the birthplace of ASI, the Barbados chapter serves as the international headquarters. It oversees all global operations and coordinates the annual "Mission 2000 and Beyond" launch.

Email: asibarbadoschapter@gmail.com

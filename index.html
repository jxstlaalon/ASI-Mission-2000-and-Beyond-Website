About ASI Mission 2000 and Beyond

ASI Mission 2000 and Beyond is a registered mission group under the Inter American Division of Seventh-day Adventist. This group was formed in the year 2000 and is currently involved in mission projects and spreading the gospel.

The group is coordinated by adults but all ministry is done by the youths particularly youth ages 13 - 25. We specialize in developing youths who haven't yet discovered their gifts, transforming shy youth into confident public speakers, and cultivating talents in puppetry, sign language, choir, art, and more.
